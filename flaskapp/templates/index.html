Welcome to my Ski Trip Optimizer site!

With the creation of many multi-mountain passes like Epic, Ikon, Mountain Collective, and many more, buying the cheapest combination of ski tickets for a season is more difficult than it used to be.

Here, we try to automate that process for you!

On this site, you can:

  1. Search through all of the ski resorts in the U.S.
  2. Select the ski resorts you plan to ski at in a season
    • Set the days and the people who are going on each trip
  3. Save your trips by logging in with a new username
  4. Run the optimizer to find how to buy your tickets/passes to determine the cheapest option {UNIMPLEMENTED}

To get started, search for your resorts!
